## How to Write a Strong Resume Header

Your header should include:

- Full name
- Phone number
- Professional email
- LinkedIn profile
- Portfolio link (if available)

### Example

**John Smith**  
johnsmith@email.com  
+91 9876543210  
linkedin.com/in/johnsmith

Keep your contact section simple and professional.
